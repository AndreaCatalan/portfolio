INTEREST

Content Creation

When I was 17, I started my first gaming channel named YUMMMYYY BG (ohmami) which focuses on the game, Blockman Go. At that time, I didn't expect much—I just wanted to try something new.


In 2021, when I was 18, I reached 100k subscribers and earned a YouTube Silver Play Button. This was one of my greatest achievements as a teenager, especially that I reached this milestone through my own hard work while also creating happy memories during the pandemic.


The channel became inactive when I started college. Now, I still love creating content, but this time is horror games on TikTok. I'm also going to try doing mini life vlog soon.

Building Beautiful Applications

I spent my almost two years in college learning back-end development. Then, just five months before my third year, I discovered my true passion: front-end development.


A YouTuber friend of mine asked if I had any experience in web development because his sister needed a website for her business. Although I was hesitant since I had never taken web development seriously, I accepted the project for free.


After completing the project, his sister was thrilled with the result. At that moment, I knew I wanted to pursue application development because I truly enjoy creating and designing beautiful applications.
